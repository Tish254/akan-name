What are Akan Names?

The Akan community in Ghana often names children according to the day of the week they were born. The names given are as follows:

Male

  • Kwasi
  • Kwadwo
  • Kwabena
  • Kwaku
  • Yaw
  • Kofi
  • Kwame

Female

  • Akosua
  • Adwoa
  • Abenaa
  • Akua
  • Yaa
  • Afua
  • Ama
